Three Different types of Referees:

  • Chief Referee
  • Water Referee
  • Timer-keeper/Scorer

Water Referee

  • There are two are three water referees
  • Water referees are on right and left side of play, with one tailing play if there are three referees
  • Use signals to signal goals, infrigemtns, penaltys, ect...

Chief Referee

  • There is one chief referee, that stands on side of pool
  • Watch teams at restarts
  • Watches substitutions
  • Watches for all surface infringements
  • Copies signals from water referees clearly, so that players can see them

Time-keeper/Scorer

  • Can be multiple people, but is usualy just one
  • Starting timer for penalised player
  • Recording score
  • Stoping match clock during last two minutes of game when there is a break, except after goal is scored
