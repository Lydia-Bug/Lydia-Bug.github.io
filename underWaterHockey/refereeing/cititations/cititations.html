Cititations

Citations are used to punish, and discourage infringing players. Citations include, verbal cautions, time penalties, and total dismissals. What citations is given depends on whether it was an repeated infringement, accidental, or deliberate, and whether it was a minor, dangerous infringement.

Caution

Verbal cautions are used for minor or accidental infringements, or when a player/team might not be aware of how they infringed.

Time Penalty

Time penalties of either one, two, or five minutes are given for repeated minor infringements, or major infringements.
If a player is in the penalty box because of time penalty then they must play with one less player until the time penalty is finished.
The time penalty for the player starts when they are seated in the penalty box and the game has restarted.

Total Dismissal

Total dismissal means that the player can not play for the rest of the game.
Total dismissals are given for deliberate major infringements, or repeated major infrigments.
If someone has been dismissed from the game then the team must play with one less player for the rest of the game.
