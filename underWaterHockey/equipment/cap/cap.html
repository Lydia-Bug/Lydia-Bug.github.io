Caps

Caps are worn by all players to identify them as an individual, and as part of their team. Caps are also used to provide ear protection.

Requirements

  • Must have a unique number from 1-14 that no one else in the team has
  • Must be either black(dark blue) or white, and match the color you are playing as
  • Must have ear protection
  • Ear protection must be firm polythylene or rubber
