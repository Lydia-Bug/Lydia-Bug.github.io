Masks

A mask is worn by all players, that covers the eyes, and nose. This makes seeing underwater a lot easier, and protects the players from headshots.

Requirements

  • Must have a dual lens design, or have a center divider/support that is part of the frame of the mask
  • Lens must be made out of safty glass, or other safty material
  • Lens can't be made out of glass
  • No metal, or metal buckles
  • Do sharp edges
