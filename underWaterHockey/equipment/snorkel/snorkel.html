Snorkels

Snorkels are used by all players to being able to watch the game, while still being able to breath. Snorkels are normally attached to the mask, but can also be attached to the cap.

Requirements

  • No sharp edges
  • Can't be made out of metal
  • Must be pliable / be able to flex
