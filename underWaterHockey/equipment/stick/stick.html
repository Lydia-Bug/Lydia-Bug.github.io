Sticks

Sticks are small sticks that push the puck along the bottom of the pool. There are many different styles of sticks, for different playing styles.

Requirements

  • Must not protude from the hand by more then 25mm
  • Must be black or white; matching the color you are playing as
  • May have markings in contrasting colors for identification purposes if not distracting or confusing

More complicated requirements

If you are buying a stick and not making one your self you don't have to worry about these requirements

  • Must be made from wood, plastic, or approved material
  • Must be able to fit, laying flat in a box that is 100mm wide x 350mm long x 50mm deep
  • Must have a corner radius of at least 10mm
  • Must be considered safe play
  • Edges must be rounded
  • Must not be capable or surrounding the puck or any part of hand
  • Must not be able to encapsulate puck by more then 50%
  • Must not protude from the hand by more then 25mm
  • Must be black or white
  • May have markings in contrasting colors for identification purposes if not distracting
