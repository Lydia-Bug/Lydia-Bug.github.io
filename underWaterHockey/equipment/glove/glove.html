Gloves

A glove must be be worn to protect the players hand, from the bottom of the pool, and the puck. Players must wear a glove on their hand which is holding the stick, and they may also wear another glove on their other hand.

Requirements

  • No webbing between fingers
  • Must not aid in swimming
  • No rigid or sharp reinforcing material
  • Must strongly contrast wit the color of the players stick and the color of the puck being used
  • Must not be bright orange, pink, or yellow
