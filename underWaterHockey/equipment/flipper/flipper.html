Flippers

Flippers, also known as fins. Are worn by all under water hockey players, so they can swim faster and reach the bottom of the pool faster.

Requirements

  • No sharp edges
  • No metal, or metal buckles
  • Can't be mae out of fiberglass
  • Must be made out of soft material or have sift material along edges; such as rubber
  • Must be able to flex
